About Weazzar

Your go-to app for real-time weather from anywhere in the world. Get accurate, up-to-date conditions plus a 7-day forecast with a clean, modern interface that adapts its theme to the actual weather around you.

✨ Features

  • Real-time temperature with feels-like reading
  • 7-day daily forecast with high/low and rain chance
  • °C / °F toggle — preference saved automatically
  • UV index, pressure, wind speed & direction, humidity, and visibility
  • Auto-detects your location on page load
  • Live city autocomplete with instant suggestions
  • Interactive location map for every search
  • Dynamic background theme that adapts to the weather condition
  • Installable as a PWA — works offline for static pages

🚀 How to Use

  1. Allow location access for automatic local weather on load
  2. Type a city name — suggestions appear after 2 characters
  3. Click a suggestion or press Enter to search
  4. Use the °C / °F toggle in the top-right of the weather card
  5. Scroll the forecast row to see the full 7-day outlook

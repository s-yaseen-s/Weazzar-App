Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026. This policy explains what data Weazzar accesses and how it is used.

📍 Location Data

If you grant permission, Weazzar uses your device's GPS coordinates solely to show local weather on page load. Your coordinates are sent directly to WeatherAPI to fetch weather data. Weazzar never stores your location.

🔒 What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect or store any personal information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not sell or share data with any third party beyond what is necessary to return weather results

🌐 Third-Party Services

  • WeatherAPI (weatherapi.com) — receives your city name or GPS coordinates to return weather and forecast data. Subject to WeatherAPI's own privacy policy.
  • Geoapify (geoapify.com) — receives coordinates to generate a static map image. Subject to Geoapify's own privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — the Poppins typeface is loaded from Google's servers. Subject to Google's privacy policy.

💾 Local Storage

Weazzar stores your temperature unit preference (°C or °F) in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's site data.
